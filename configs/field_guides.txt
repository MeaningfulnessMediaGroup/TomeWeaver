[HELP:TITLE]
This field dictates the overarching name of your adventure. It helps the AI understand the core theme and sets the folder name on your computer.

[EXAMPLE:TITLE]
ALL: The Heist of the Ruby Skull
ALL: Echoes of the Leviathan
SANDBOX: Mallow the Cat
SANDBOX: Lost in the Neon City
SANDBOX: The Merchant of Driftwood
CAMPAIGN: The Last Train to Oakhaven
CAMPAIGN: Project: Icarus
CAMPAIGN: The Bloodline Crown



[HELP:CHAR]
This field is used to describe the main character's features. The AI performs best when you include physical limitations, quirky personality traits, and internal fears alongside basic descriptions.

[EXAMPLE:CHAR]
ALL: Kaelen, a cynical thief with a bad knee and a penchant for gambling.
ALL: Subject 84, an amnesiac supersoldier who is terrifyingly fast but deeply paranoid.
SANDBOX: Bob the builder, 27yo, short but muscular, lives in his parents' attic after a failed marriage.
SANDBOX: Elara, a starry-eyed potion merchant with a silver tongue and a terrible sense of direction.
SANDBOX: Barnaby, an overly enthusiastic golden retriever separated from his owner.
CAMPAIGN: Vera, 21yo, an art major having an existential crisis about her career prospects.
CAMPAIGN: Captain Vance, a disgraced naval officer looking for one last shot at redemption.
CAMPAIGN: Silas the Grim, an elderly necromancer who is severely allergic to dust.

ALL: Epic high-fantasy, heroic, grand, awe-inspiring.
ALL: Post-apocalyptic, bleak, desperate, lonely, atmospheric.
ALL: Cinematic, explosive, fast-paced, adrenaline-fueled action.
ALL: Mythic, fable-like, enchanting, lyrical, fairytale.
ALL: Industrial steampunk, clockwork, smog-filled, rebellious.

ALL: Slice-of-life, mundane, character-driven, emotional, grounded.

[HELP:GOAL]
The overarching motivation driving the protagonist. In Campaign mode, this is the final "end game" objective. In Sandbox mode, this gives the AI a reason to push the story forward rather than stagnating.

[EXAMPLE:GOAL]
ALL: Escape the facility before the self-destruct timer hits zero.
ALL: Survive the harsh wilderness and find a way back to civilization.
SANDBOX: Find a comfortable place to sleep and maybe a bowl of milk.
SANDBOX: Build a thriving tavern business from the ground up.
SANDBOX: Explore the infinite procedural backrooms and document anomalies.
SANDBOX: Ascend the ranks of the local thieves guild by picking pockets and making connections.
CAMPAIGN: Steal the Ruby Skull from the Sunken Tomb and escape the city.
CAMPAIGN: Assassinate the corrupt King before the royal wedding concludes.
CAMPAIGN: Deliver the encrypted datapad to the rebel base across the galaxy.
CAMPAIGN: Find a cure for the supernatural plague ravaging your village.



[HELP:SETTING]
The initial environment where the story begins. Focus on sensory details: lighting, architecture, weather, and smells. The AI loves rich environmental descriptions.

[EXAMPLE:SETTING]
ALL: A dense, spooky forest at midnight. The air smells like wet pine and ozone.
ALL: An abandoned sci-fi research facility on a frozen moon. Blaring red alarms and the hiss of broken steam pipes.
ALL: A crowded, dirty tavern in the slums of Oakhaven. Smells like cheap ale and sweat.
SANDBOX: A bustling interstellar space station market filled with alien merchants and neon signs.
SANDBOX: A seemingly endless office building with yellow wallpaper and buzzing fluorescent lights.
SANDBOX: A cozy, sunlit cottage kitchen filled with the smell of baking bread and dried herbs.
CAMPAIGN: The rainy, cobblestone streets of a Victorian-era London analogue, lit by flickering gas lamps.
CAMPAIGN: A moving bullet-train speeding through a desolate, irradiated wasteland.
CAMPAIGN: The grand, marble foyer of a vampire lord's castle, chillingly silent and draped in red velvet.



[HELP:LORE]
Hard rules the AI must never break. Use this to ban certain technologies, define magic systems, set physical laws, or limit the protagonist's abilities.

[EXAMPLE:LORE]
ALL: Magic is highly illegal and punishable by death.
ALL: Zombies are completely blind but have incredible hearing.
ALL: The protagonist is a normal cat. He cannot speak English or hold items in his paws.
ALL: All computer networks are infected by a hostile AI; manual analog tools are the only safe option.
ALL: The sun never sets in this realm. It is constantly blazing noon.
ALL: Anyone who speaks a lie immediately coughs up a black rose petal.



[HELP:COLD_OPEN]
Sets the immediate context for Turn 1. What is the protagonist doing right this exact second? Put them right in the middle of the action!

[EXAMPLE:COLD_OPEN]
ALL: Waking up in a cryo-pod with alarms blaring and no memory of how I got here.
ALL: Crouched behind a wooden barrel, hiding from a patrolling guard.
ALL: Sitting at the bar, nursing a cheap ale, when a stranger approaches with a map.
SANDBOX: Stepping off the bus into a strange, rainy city with only a suitcase and ten dollars.
SANDBOX: Staring at a blank computer screen in a cubicle, feeling an overwhelming urge to run away.
SANDBOX: Opening the front door to find a mysterious, ticking package on the porch.
CAMPAIGN: Dangling by one hand from the ledge of a skyscraper, hearing the police sirens approach.
CAMPAIGN: Standing over the body of a corrupt official, holding a smoking pistol.
CAMPAIGN: Strapped to an interrogation chair as the villain walks in holding a pair of pliers.



[HELP:CHAP_GOAL]
The specific, actionable objective for this exact chapter. The AI will not allow the chapter to end until the player successfully achieves this goal.

[EXAMPLE:CHAP_GOAL]
CAMPAIGN: Find a way to sneak past or bribe the city guards to enter Oakhaven.
CAMPAIGN: Break into the manor, locate the hidden safe, and steal the Ruby Skull.
CAMPAIGN: Navigate the spooky forest and find the hidden ruins without being caught by the mercenaries.
CAMPAIGN: Interrogate the captured smuggler and extract the password to the mainframe.
CAMPAIGN: Successfully navigate the high-society gala and quietly poison the Duke's wine.
CAMPAIGN: Survive the first wave of alien attackers and restore power to the communication array.



[HELP:CHAP_OBSTACLES]
Specific threats or challenges that prevent the player from easily achieving the goal. The AI will use these to block the player's path and create tension.

[EXAMPLE:CHAP_OBSTACLES]
CAMPAIGN: The guards are highly alert and you have no money for a bribe.
CAMPAIGN: Locked doors, patrolling guard dogs, and trap runes on the windows.
CAMPAIGN: Slippery shingles, guards shooting crossbows, and a rival thief trying to steal the skull.
CAMPAIGN: The smuggler is stubborn, and time is running out before his gang arrives to rescue him.
CAMPAIGN: Nosy guests constantly trying to make small talk, and the Duke has a personal food taster.
CAMPAIGN: Ammunition is severely limited, and the power relay is across a dark, flooded corridor.



[HELP:CHAP_TIME]
A brief instruction for the AI to seamlessly skip time between chapters.

[EXAMPLE:CHAP_TIME]
CAMPAIGN: Later that night...
CAMPAIGN: The next morning, as the sun rises...
CAMPAIGN: Three days later, deep in the mountains...
CAMPAIGN: Immediately after. No time skip.
CAMPAIGN: A week later, after recovering from the wounds...
CAMPAIGN: Flashback to ten years ago...



[HELP:INVENTORY]

To use an example from below, click it. The AI will automatically read the example string and construct the full UI Schema (complete with Icons and Hex Colors) for you!

ALL: Health: 100/100. Stamina: High. Weapon: Plasma Rifle. Credits: 50.
ALL: Sanity: Deteriorating. Fear: High. Clues: Torn photograph.
ALL: Hunger: Starving. Thirst: Parched. Backpack: Empty.
ALL: Armor: Broken. Main Hand: Longsword. Off Hand: Wooden Shield.
SANDBOX: Gold: 15. Reputation: Unknown. Cargo: 3x Spice crates.
CAMPAIGN: Stealth: Undetected. Noise Level: Low. Tools: Lockpicks.
