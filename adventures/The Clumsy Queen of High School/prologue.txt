The sun was just beginning to peek through Livvy's curtains when she suddenly jolted awake. Her heart raced as she glanced at the clock - 7:30 AM! She had overslept on the first day of school, and now she only had an hour to get ready and make it to homeroom on time.

Livvy leaped out of bed, tripping over a pile of clothes in her rush. 'Great,' she muttered, 'and I still have to do my hair...' She stumbled into the bathroom, her long brown hair sticking up at odd angles from a restless night's sleep. As she brushed it back into place, she caught sight of herself in the mirror and sighed. Her green eyes were rimmed with dark circles from staying up late finishing her summer reading assignment - a classic case of procrastination.

'Where are my shoes?' Livvy mumbled to herself as she rummaged through her closet. She found one sneaker, then the other, but in her haste, she dropped them both and watched helplessly as they skidded across the hardwood floor. 'Just perfect,' she grumbled, picking them up again.

Livvy grabbed her backpack, double-checking to make sure she had everything she needed for the day. Her notebooks were stuffed haphazardly inside, along with a half-eaten granola bar and a crumpled list of class schedules. She took one last look in the mirror before heading out the door, hoping that today would be different - that maybe this year, people wouldn't see her as 'that clumsy girl' anymore.
