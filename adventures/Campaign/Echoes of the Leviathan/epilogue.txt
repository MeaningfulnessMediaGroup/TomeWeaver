Elara collapsed into the escape pod's seat, gasping for breath and shaking uncontrollably as adrenaline coursed through her system. The ship's self-destruct sequence ticked down towards zero, red lights flickering along the walls. She had done it - somehow defied all odds to survive this nightmare, but at what cost? Her hands were still slick with an alien substance she didn't want to identify, and she could feel a dozen new bruises forming from her desperate sprint through the ship's corridors. As the pod detached and rocketed away into the void, she allowed herself one final look back at the Leviathan. The once-mighty vessel was now nothing more than a floating tomb, slowly expanding in a cataclysmic burst of light as its engines overloaded. In that moment, Elara knew one thing for certain: no matter where life took her next, she would never forget the echoes of the dead.
