The darkness was total, suffocating. Elara felt herself floating in zero gravity, heart pounding against her ribcage like a caged bird desperate for freedom. The cryo-bay around her was silent save for the faint hum of dormant machinery and the distant sound of something moving through the ductwork above - skittering sounds that raised gooseflesh on her skin. Her oxygen levels were critically low, the small tank attached to her cryo-suit hissing as it struggled to maintain a viable atmosphere. She had to move. Had to find a way out before she suffocated or faced whatever was lurking in the shadows.
